## Team-404

Our team is made up of 6 students working on our final year project, the WiFinder, a device that can assist with tracking, mapping and monitoring wireless networks for use in tracking malicious networks and identifying compromised devices or simply auditing wifi signal strength.

## Hardware list
- Raspberry Pi 4B 4Gb Flashed with Raspbian Bookworm
- 5 inch HDMI GPIO Touchscreen (Jaycar - XC9024, Waveshare - 14300)
- USB Wireless interface that supports monitor mode (TPLink - wn722n)
- Directional 2.4Ghz/5Ghz antenna
- Portable charger or battery pack
- 3D printed case (No offical case created yet)
